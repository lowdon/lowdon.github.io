Lode Runner

Lode Runner is a 2D, fixed-screen platform game video game first published by Broderbund in 1983. Levels in Lode Runner are puzzle-oriented. The player controls one character who must collect all the gold pieces in a level and then get to the end while being chased by a number of enemies.

Have fun!
